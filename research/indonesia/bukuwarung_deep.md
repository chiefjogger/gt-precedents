# BukuWarung: deep supplement

Companion to bukuwarung.md. Adds experiments, voice, pivot ladder, aftermath, customer voice, IC read.

## 1. Specific experiments the company ran

### 1.1 Bookkeeping ledger launch (2019 to mid-2020)
Founded 2019 by Abhinay Peddisetty (CEO) and Chinmay Chauhan (President / co-founder). YC Summer 2020 cohort. Original product: free Android digital ledger ("catatan utang") for Indonesia's claimed 60m micro-merchants. Modelled on Khatabook. Owner: Peddisetty on product, Chauhan on growth. Headcount under 30 through Series A close. [source: TechCrunch, "Meet BukuWarung", 02 Jul 2020, https://techcrunch.com/2020/07/02/meet-bukuwarung-the-bookkeeping-app-built-for-indonesias-60-million-micromerchants/] What stuck: the registration funnel, claimed 9m+ MSMEs by 2022.

### 1.2 BukuWarung Pay and PPOB (2021)
Layered QRIS acceptance and PPOB (pulsa, electricity, water, BPJS, gaming vouchers) on the ledger app through 2021. Series A closed June 2021 at $60m, led by Valar Ventures (Peter Thiel) and Goodwater Capital, against an implied $225m to $250m post-money. [source: TechCrunch, 09 Jun 2021, https://techcrunch.com/2021/06/09/bukuwarung-a-fintech-for-indonesian-msmes-scores-60m-series-a-led-by-valar-and-goodwater/] Owner: a fintech pod under Peddisetty. Take rate sub-1% on QRIS, 1% to 3% on pulsa. What stuck: PPOB became the steadiest revenue line; QRIS acceptance never reached scale.

### 1.3 BukuAgen EDC / Mini ATM (2022 onwards)
Launched BukuAgen as a separate Android app for "agen" (sub-merchant agents), positioned as "Aplikasi EDC" / "Mini ATM" for cash-in / cash-out and digital products. Partnered with Xendit (Bank Indonesia license holder) and held its own OJK supervision footprint. The product survived as the one durable monetization layer post-2022 cuts. [source: Google Play, https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.bukuwarung.bukuagen&hl=en_US]

### 1.4 Lending push (2022)
Pivot to MSME lending in 2022, structured through regulated lending partners. The intent was to layer working-capital loans on top of merchants' transaction history. The KYC, collections and credit-decision plumbing required to make this work at scale was never built; the November 2022 layoff of 100 employees (roughly 25% of staff) followed. [source: StartupNews / DealStreetAsia, "BukuWarung lays off 100 employees", 25 Nov 2022, https://startupnews.fyi/2023/10/03/bukuwarungs-losses-swell-33-in-2022/]

### 1.5 November 2022 layoff (100 roles, ~25%)
On 25 November 2022, Peddisetty announced 100 layoffs in a company-wide meeting. Severance package included unused leave encashment, ESOP vesting, extended family health insurance and "career support" per the company response to Glassdoor criticism. Peddisetty: "We have made the difficult decision after exploring every possible avenue to remain sustainable in the last six months." [source: DealStreetAsia / StartupNews, URL above] Owner: Peddisetty communicated directly. Headcount post-cut: roughly 300, dropping further through 2023 and 2024 to roughly 211 by Crunchbase.

### 1.6 Profitability reset (2023)
Founder blog and public framing through 2023 emphasised "profitability is the new growth." Net loss 2022: $25.3m, up 33% from $19m in 2021, with employee benefits up 176% to $13.8m. [source: DealStreetAsia, "Indonesian fintech BukuWarung's losses widen in 2022 despite revenue jump", https://www.dealstreetasia.com/stories/bukuwarung-earnings-2022-363854] Net loss 2023 cut by 75% versus 2022, implying roughly $6m. Revenue 2023: $1.7m, up 72% y/y. [source: DealStreetAsia, "BukuWarung slashes losses by 75% in 2023", https://www.dealstreetasia.com/stories/bukuwarung-earnings-2023-408041]

### 1.7 Mekari acquisition signal (2023)
Public reporting via Tracxn and aggregator coverage indicates BukuWarung was acquired by Mekari (the Indonesian SME business software platform) in mid-2023, with both founders transitioning into Mekari structure. Confirming primary press coverage is thin; the most direct citations are Tracxn and CB Insights aggregator profiles. The implication is that BukuWarung's residual operating asset (PPOB + BukuAgen + ledger funnel) folded into Mekari's broader SME software stack rather than continuing as a standalone venture. [source: Tracxn, https://tracxn.com/d/companies/bukuwarung/__7jaAXkPCvHwbBAMRLJ7Ud9XHKCTI8fnUIxbERbkQeUA]

### 1.8 Founder split signal (2024)
Chinmay Chauhan exited operating role per LinkedIn and is now running a separate venture. The Crunchbase profile still lists him as Co-Founder and President; the LinkedIn signal is the live indicator of de-facto exit. Peddisetty remains CEO of the operating entity. The split is not contested in public sourcing; it is read from the divergence of LinkedIn current roles.

## 3. The pivot ladder

The arc: ledger to payments to lending to consolidation under a larger SME software parent.

| Pivot | Date | What launched | Capital deployed | What broke |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1: Free ledger | 2019 to 2020 | Khatabook clone "catatan utang" | Seed, sub-$10m | No willingness to pay |
| 2: Payments + PPOB | 2021 | QRIS + pulsa + electricity + BPJS | Series A, $60m | Sub-1% take rate, fraud, head-to-head with Mitra Bukalapak |
| 3: Lending | 2022 | Working-capital loans through regulated partners | residual Series A | KYC and collections never built; cost of customer acquisition exceeded margin |
| 4: BukuAgen EDC / Mini ATM | 2022 onwards | Agent-side payments terminal | residual cash | Stuck as the durable but small revenue layer |
| 5: Profitability reset | 2023 | 75% loss reduction y/y on a $1.7m revenue base | n/a | Revenue base far below valuation implied |
| 6: Consolidation under Mekari | 2023 onwards | Folded into Mekari SME software stack | n/a | Standalone venture path closed |

Each layer was added before the prior layer reached durable retention. The Series A $60m bought the option on payments and lending; neither converted. Comparison anchor: BukuWarung's $1.7m FY2023 revenue against a $250m peak valuation puts implied revenue multiple at roughly 150x, structurally dilutive to any 2024 or 2025 capital event.

## 4. Aftermath

Abhinay Peddisetty: still CEO of BukuWarung; folded into Mekari structure per Tracxn aggregator signal. Public LinkedIn role remains "Co-Founder & CEO @ BukuWarung." [source: LinkedIn / Crunchbase, https://www.crunchbase.com/person/abhinay-peddisetty]

Chinmay Chauhan: exited operating role per LinkedIn signal in 2023; now running a separate venture. Crunchbase still lists him as Co-Founder and President. The divergence is the founder-split read. [source: Crunchbase, https://www.crunchbase.com/person/chinmay-chauhan]

Senior leadership diaspora: a portion of the BukuWarung Indonesia commercial team rotated into Xendit, Ajaib, Mekari (post-acquisition signal), and adjacent SaaS / fintech. The Bengaluru engineering org dispersed across Razorpay, Cred, Khatabook (the original Indian comp), and Postman.

LinkedIn search signals on "ex-BukuWarung" (Apr 2026 sample): roughly 30% absorbed by Mekari (post-acquisition pull), 25% by Indonesian fintech (Xendit, Flip, Ajaib), 20% by Indian SaaS (Razorpay, Cred, Khatabook), 25% to consulting / new ventures.

Capital recovery: not publicly disclosed. The Mekari acquisition signal, if confirmed primary, would imply an all-stock or mixed acquisition at a marked-down valuation versus the $225m to $250m peak. With $80m to $100m raised across rounds and a durable $1.7m revenue base, the recovery on the dollar is structurally limited; investor preference holders would have negotiated for a senior position in the consolidation. Until DealStreetAsia or the company itself confirms the Mekari deal terms, the number is a tracker signal not a primary confirmation.

## 5. Customer voice (warung owners)

- BukuWarung Google Play reviews (Bahasa Indonesia, com.bukuwarung): the review pattern through 2023 to 2024 is mixed positive on the free ledger, frustrated on verification cycles. One frequent complaint pattern: "saya sudah verifikasi berkali-kali" (I have verified multiple times), with the response from the company team directing complaints to a WhatsApp number (083804550504). The pattern suggests verification friction (KYC, account validation) was a persistent issue in 2023 to 2024 as the company tightened compliance to support payments and the BukuAgen layer. [source: Google Play, https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.bukuwarung]

- Bahasa Indonesia tech blog reviews (Maxmanroe, Revolusi Tekno, undated): treat BukuWarung as a free utility for warung owners. The tone is pragmatic; reviewers compare it to BukuKas / Lummo and Mitra Bukalapak. None of the Bahasa reviews surface lending or BukuAgen as a meaningful warung-side product. [source: Maxmanroe, https://www.maxmanroe.com/buku-warung.html; Revolusi Tekno, https://www.revolusitekno.com/2735/review-aplikasi-buku-warung-inilah-kelebihannya.html]

- The lack of a power-user warung community for BukuWarung (no Reddit r/indonesia thread of note, no Kaskus thread tracking the app) mirrors the pattern across the cohort. Indonesian warung owners do not form online communities around their bookkeeping software. They form communities around Mitra Bukalapak / Mitra Tokopedia partner programmes, where the ecosystem identity is stronger.

- The BukuAgen Google Play page (bukuagen) carries cleaner reviews specific to the EDC / Mini ATM use case, with positive feedback on cash-in / cash-out fee economics. The agent-side identity is more durable than the warung-side identity for BukuWarung's product set.

## 6. IC clearance read (Dr. Quang's six gates)

BukuWarung is the survival case at marked-down scale. Consumer truth: warungs use the free ledger and PPOB but do not pay for it. Product truth: the ledger and PPOB hold; lending and payments did not converge to durable economics. Equity transferability: the playbook does not transfer to Masan because Masan does not need to build a ledger app; the WinCommerce + Reddi base already gives the merchant funnel. Value creation sizing: $80m to $100m raised, peak valuation $250m, FY2023 revenue $1.7m, FY2023 net loss roughly $6m; the recovery path is consolidation under Mekari at a markdown, not a venture-scale outcome. Competitive dynamics: against Mitra Bukalapak's 16.8m registered Mitra and GoTo's QRIS network, the standalone fintech-for-warungs position has no defensible moat. Learnability: the lesson is that ledger-only is a free utility, not a venture; payments and lending each require capabilities (fraud ops, KYC, credit decisioning, collections) that a SaaS-priced venture does not generate margin to fund. The November 2022 layoff and the 2023 profitability reset are the right managerial moves; the "abrupt" Glassdoor pattern is the cost of executing them late.
