## Methodology notes

- Where a number could not be verified to a primary source, the closest sourced estimate is flagged inline in the company file, with the qualifier 'Closest sourced estimate' or 'Not publicly disclosed; signals point to'.
- Funding totals draw on Tracxn, PitchBook, and DealStreetAsia capital tracker triangulation; trackers occasionally disagree on undisclosed-amount rounds (e.g. Wahyoo, Dagangan); both readings are captured where present.
- Vietnamese-language sources (CafeF, VnExpress, BaoDauTu, ViettTimes, Bao Chinh Phu, etc.) are cited with the original Vietnamese title in italics where the article carries one, and the rough English translation in the surrounding prose.
- The Singapore High Court ruling on OOPA Pte Ltd v Bui Sy Phong is cited via Mondaq's 2021 summary; the underlying judgment is in the Singapore Supreme Court records but not freely available online.
- App store reviews and LinkedIn employee posts are cited where they materially inform the failure-mode analysis. Anonymised LinkedIn quotes carry the qualifier 'anonymised' and a hash placeholder; full attribution is held in internal notes only.

## Excluded sources

Per the research brief, the following source classes were avoided:

- Company-published case studies and vendor blogs.
- Content farms and AI-generated SEO articles.
- Unverified Substack and Medium posts unless the author has a direct operating connection (e.g. The Runway Ventures, ex-operator commentary).
